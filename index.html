About Me

Hey there! I'm Morgan Hamlin, and I'm training to be a full-stack developer through Georgia Tech, with a specialization in CSS and Javascript. After graduating from Florida State University with a B.S. in Environmental Science, I began working as a communications intern at the Grant Park Conservancy. It was through that internship that I discovered my love for web development. I hope to find work with a web design firm, or a marketing agency that provides both a challenging and supportive workspace.
When I'm not building webites, you can find me exploring the city I love the most- Atlanta.
Truth is, websites can be tricky! That's why I love building them. Check out my portfolio to view some of my work!
